Force Field Pro Shader
Code-Phi 2017

INSTRUCTIONS:
Just use shader "ForceField" on any of you materials and modify the parameters:

Bump Texture - used for image distortion and bumpmapping
Bump Normal - normalmap
Base Color - main color of materials
Intersecion Color - color applied when shader intersects other abjects
Intersection Threshold - size of intersection area
Distorition - amount of image distortion applied to materials
Bump Strength - bumpmapping intensity
Silhouette Enchancement - controls the intensity and look of rim around shader
X/Y Speed - animation speed in uv space
